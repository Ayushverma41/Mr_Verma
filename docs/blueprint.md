## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Blue-Gray (#343a40) to establish a professional and modern aesthetic.
- Background color: Black (#000000) provides a strong contrast, allowing content to stand out.
- Accent color: Light Gray (#d3d3d3) used for text and subtle details to maintain readability and visual interest without overwhelming the design.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font, for a modern and readable style across the portfolio.
- Use minimalist icons for skills and project categories to provide quick visual cues.
- Employ a grid-based layout to ensure a structured and responsive design across different screen sizes.
- Subtle fade-in animations on scroll to enhance user experience and engagement.
